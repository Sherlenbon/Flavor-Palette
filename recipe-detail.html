Spaghetti Carbonara

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Pancetta - 100g
  • Parmesan cheese - 50g
  • Eggs - 2

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine everything and serve.
