Tiramisu

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese: 250g
  • Heavy cream: 200ml
  • Espresso coffee: 200ml
  • Ladyfingers: 12 pieces
  • Cocoa powder: for dusting
  • Sugar: 50g
  • Dark chocolate shavings: for garnish

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso and let it cool.
  2. Whip the heavy cream and sugar until soft peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone cheese.
  4. Dip the ladyfingers quickly into the espresso and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top.
  6. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used up.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
