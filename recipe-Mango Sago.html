Mango Sago

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Mango: 2 ripe
  • Sago pearls: 50g
  • Coconut milk: 200ml
  • Condensed milk: 50ml
  • Ice cubes: optional
  • Fresh mint leaves: for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the sago pearls in boiling water until translucent. Rinse under cold water and set aside.
  2. Blend one mango with coconut milk and condensed milk until smooth.
  3. Dice the second mango into small cubes.
  4. Combine the sago pearls, mango puree, and mango cubes in a serving bowl.
  5. Add ice cubes if desired and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  6. Serve chilled.
