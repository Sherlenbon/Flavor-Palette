Kung Pao Chicken

  • Chicken breast: 300g
  • Dried red chilies: 6
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1 small piece, minced
  • Peanuts: 50g
  1. Cut chicken into cubes and marinate with soy sauce and cornstarch.
  2. Heat oil, stir-fry chilies, then add chicken.
  3. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, green onions, and sauce mixture.
  4. Add peanuts and serve hot.
