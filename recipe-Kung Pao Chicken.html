Kung Pao Chicken

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast: 300g
  • Dried red chilies: 6
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1 small piece, minced
  • Peanuts: 50g
  • Green onions: 3 stalks, chopped
  • Soy sauce: 3 tbsp
  • Hoisin sauce: 1 tbsp
  • Vinegar: 1 tbsp
  • Sugar: 1 tsp
  • Cornstarch: 1 tbsp
  • Water: 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized cubes and marinate with 1 tbsp soy sauce and cornstarch for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry dried red chilies until aromatic.
  3. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, and green onions, stir-frying for a minute.
  5. Mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water in a bowl, and pour into the wok.
  6. Add peanuts and stir well to coat everything in the sauce.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
