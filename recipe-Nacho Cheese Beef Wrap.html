Nacho Cheese Beef Wrap

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: 200g
  • Taco seasoning: 1 packet
  • Nacho cheese sauce: 1/2 cup
  • Flour tortillas: 2 large
  • Tortilla chips: 1/2 cup (crushed)
  • Optional toppings: Lettuce, diced tomatoes, sour cream, jalape√±os

Instructions

  1. Cook the Beef: Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water, then cook until well coated and fragrant.
  3. Heat the Cheese: Warm the nacho cheese sauce in a microwave or small saucepan until smooth and creamy.
  4. Assemble the Wrap: Place a tortilla flat. Add seasoned beef, warm cheese sauce, and crushed tortilla chips in the center. Optionally, layer with lettuce, tomatoes, or other toppings.
  5. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll tightly from the bottom.
  6. Serve: Slice in half if desired and enjoy immediately.
